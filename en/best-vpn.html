My Testing Method: No Marketing BS

Real Tests, Not Paid Promotions

I pay for subscriptions myself—no demo accounts or freebies from VPN companies. Every service gets connected to 10-15 different servers, speed-tested via Speedtest.net, and leak-checked through ipleak.net. If it fails my tests, it doesn't make the list.

Real-World Usage, Not Just Benchmarks

Speedtest numbers are nice, but I test what you'll actually use: streaming 4K on Netflix, downloading 50GB torrents, gaming in Warzone while checking ping. If a VPN cuts your speed by more than 30% or causes lag spikes in games, it gets marked down in my review.

Privacy Verification, Not Marketing Claims

Everyone claims 'no-logs,' but few pass independent audits. I check jurisdiction (14 Eyes = bad, Panama/Cyprus = good), look for cases of data handovers to authorities, and read actual auditor reports. If a company lies in their privacy policy once, they're blacklisted permanently.
